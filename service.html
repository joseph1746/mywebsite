Our Services

I. Core Services

At DIGI DOC, we provide essential computer services designed to keep your systems running smoothly. Our Computer Repair service covers both desktop and laptop diagnostics and repair. We offer complete Virus & Malware Removal to protect your systems from threats. Our Data Recovery solutions help retrieve lost or corrupted data from hard drives, SSDs, USBs, and cloud storage. For performance enhancement, we provide Hardware Upgrades including RAM, CPU, GPU, and storage replacements. Additionally, we offer Operating System Installation and Reinstallation across Windows, macOS, and Linux platforms.

II. Value-Added Services

We go beyond the basics to offer additional services that deliver more value and convenience. Our Network Setup and Troubleshooting ensures seamless connectivity for home and small business networks. With Data Backup & Recovery Solutions, we help secure your important files and prepare for unforeseen data loss. Our technicians provide Printer Repair and Setup as well as Software Installation & Support for a range of applications. We also offer Internet and Wi-Fi Troubleshooting to fix connectivity issues and improve speed and coverage. For damaged laptops, we perform Laptop Screen Repair, and we also assist with Data Transfer between devices. To ensure convenience, we provide both On-site Service (within our service area) and fast, reliable Remote Support.

III. Specialized Services

Our team is equipped to handle advanced and niche technical needs. We offer expert Mac Repair services for Apple products. For gamers and enthusiasts, our Gaming PC Repair and Custom Builds deliver high-performance systems tailored to your needs. Businesses can rely on us for Server Maintenance, including setup, monitoring, and repairs. We also provide Data Security Consulting to help individuals and organizations strengthen their digital defenses. Additionally, we repair Mobile Devices, offering screen and hardware repairs for phones and tablets.
